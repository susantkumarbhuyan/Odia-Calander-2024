Introducing Odia Calendar by Susant Kumar

Namaskar Odisha! We are delighted to present the Odia Calendar, a meticulously crafted app designed to bring you the richness of Odia culture, traditions, and spirituality right at your fingertips.

Key Features:

  • Odisha Tithis and Festivals: Never miss a significant date again! Our app provides detailed information about Odisha Tithis and festivals, ensuring you stay connected with your roots and celebrate the essence of Odia heritage.
  • Horoscope Insights: Unlock the secrets of the stars with our comprehensive horoscope feature. Get personalized horoscope readings tailored to your zodiac sign, providing you with valuable insights into your day, week, and month ahead.
  • Lunar Calendar: Embrace the celestial rhythm with our lunar calendar, offering precise lunar phase details. Stay attuned to the lunar cycles for auspicious occasions and cultural observances.
  • Festival Alerts: Receive timely notifications about upcoming festivals and events, enabling you to plan your celebrations and participate in the vibrant tapestry of Odia festivities.
  • Sleek Jetpack Compose Design: Experience a seamless and visually appealing interface powered by Jetpack Compose. Our user-friendly design ensures a smooth and enjoyable navigation experience for users of all ages.
